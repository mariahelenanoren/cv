Där ingen ska bo – an exhibition website

A website for the exhibition "Där ingen ska bo" at Riksarkivet in Gothenburg

The exhibition "Där ingen ska bo" was a collaboration between Riksarkivet in Gothenburg and HDK, Acadamy of Art and Design. The exhibition was centered around the life at Östra Nordstan in Gothenburg during the 1960's. The website acted both as a way of presenting information regaring the exhibition as well as contributing additional materal by using open source images and excerpts from stories which emereged during the research phase. Due to my knowledge regarding coding being very limited at the time, the code is not public. The "Arkivsimulator" was created by another student.
