Tactile Communication

Accessable folders for the governments 2019 spring budget

One of the requirements for the government to follow is to make communication material available for everyone. This was something I wanted to explore by taking their 2019 budget and presenting it in a was that was tactile, rather than visual. I made a folder which was completely white but with the text written in braille and diagrams presented by structure. I also made a version with the same structures but where I also follow the recommendation put forth by the Synskadades Riksförbund about making accessable visual material.
