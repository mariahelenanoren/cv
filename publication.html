A Room of One's Own – a joint publication

A joint publication between me and six other students at HDK.

A room of one's own is an essay written by Virgina Woolf. Inspired by the first chapter, I, like the character, documented a walk to places which I am familiar with. At these familiar places I photographed printed excerpts from the book. In the publication I also added a double-sided page which giva a hint as to where the places are located.
