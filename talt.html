Tält – graphic identity

Graphic identity and printed material for the fictional tent brand "Tält".

"Tält" is a fictional tent brand created as a group project between me and three other students at HDK. For the brand I created a graphical identity inspired by the tents we designed. The brand was supposed to be modern, and market camping for a younger audience. For the printed material I created business cards and a short pamphlet which were inspired by clothing tags.
