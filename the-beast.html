The Beast – cover art

Cover art for Leif Strandh's single "The Beast".

The cover consists of a collage of different open source images. The finished result was reached by designing and discussing several different versions of the cover art with Leif. In the final version, there is a morphed image of Vladimir Putin, Recep Tayyip Erdoğan and Donald Trump in the center. Behind the character a whirlwind of different "Beasts" are depicted. In the very back different scenes between people are depicted. While a Beast can be a person, it can also be an emotion and a human flaw. Flaws which can be found within all of us.
