Understanding Design Theory

A critique of badly communicated, difficult to understand course litterature and research papers

There has probably been times where you, either willingly or reluctantly, have had to read a research paper. Not only did you have to read the paper itself, but next to you there was also a dictionary you had to stop and consult every two sentences. As a possible solution for this I recreated a paper about sustainable design, and made it into a book with a built in vocabulary for those terms and words that might be difficult for the average person to understand.
